<div><span><span><span><img src="http://ih.constantcontact.com/fs007/1102748757357/img/213.jpg" border="0" alt="MDA Pix 1" width="202" height="147">HO</span><span><strong>LLYWOOD, CA. </strong>The Detroit Free Press and Freep.coms Motown at 50 online entertainment project won <strong>Best of Show</strong> and a $500 check at the Los Angeles Press Clubs 3<sup>rd</sup> Annual National Entertainment Journalism Awards Thursday night inHollywood. </span></span></span></div>
<div>

<div>
<p><span>The best entertainment website beat out first place winners in 15 other categories, from show business reporters and media organizations across the nationfrom Alaska to Kansas City.</span></p>
<div><span>The LA Weeklys chief film critic <strong>Scott Foundas</strong>, now with the New York Film Festival, walked away with two awards, including Print Critic of the Year. KABC Channel 7s <strong>George Pennacchio</strong> also scored two wins in the TV news and feature categories. </span></div>
</div>
<div>KTLA entertainment reporter <strong>Sam Rubin </strong>was roasted by screenwriter <strong>Stephen Mazur </strong>and KTLA anchor <strong>Jessica Holmes</strong> and received the top individual award for career achievement. Last years award went to Joe Morgenstern, KCRW and Wall Street Journal film critic.</div>

</div>
<p><span><span>Below are the NEJ 2010 Winnerspresented by Los Angeles Press Club..</span></span></p>
<div>
<div>
<div>
<div>
<div><strong><span>A. PRINT</span></strong></div>

<div><span>A1. Print News </span></div>
<div><span>Winner: <strong>Kyle Hopkins</strong>, Anchorage Daily News, E.T. go Nome?</span></div>

<div><span>A2a. Print Feature or Series over 1,000 Words </span></div>

<div><span>Winner: <strong>Richard Siklos</strong>, Fortune, The Fight Over Michaels Millions</span></div>
<div><span>Second Place: Anthony Effinger and Daniel Taub, Bloomberg Markets, All Shook Up</span></div>
<div><span>Honorable Mention: Martha Sarabia, La Opinion, Telenovelas</span></div>

<div><span>A2b. Print FeaturePersonality Profile Over 1,000 words</span></div>
<div><span>Winner: <strong>Scott Foundas</strong>, L.A. Weekly, Boxing, Sex and Madness: Mike Tyson, James Toback and the Ties that Bind.</span></div>
<div><span>Second Place: Robert Gordon, Playboy, Soul Man</span></div>
<div><span>Honorable Mention: Eric Spitznagel, Playboy, Andy Richter Grows Up</span></div>

<div><span>A3. Print Feature under 1,000 Words </span></div>
<div><span>Winner: <strong>Lisa Ingrassia</strong>, People, Kelly Osbourne: I knew if I didnt get help, I would die </span></div>
<div><span>Second Place: Martha Sarabia, La Opinion, The Venezuelan Export Gustavo Dudamel</span></div>
<div><span>Honorable Mention: Jeanne Wolf, Parade, Have Faith in Something Big</span></div>

<div><span>A4. Print Critic of the Year</span></div>

<div><span>Winner:<strong>Scott Foundas</strong>, L.A. Weekly, body of work</span></div>
<div><span>Honorable Mention: Lavender Vroman, Antelope Valley Press</span></div>

<div><span>A5. Best Entertainment Publication </span></div>
<div><span>Winner:<strong>Lavender Vroman, Julie Jane Rios, Steve Galbreath</strong>, Antelope Valley Press Showcase</span></div>
<div><span>Second Place: Drex Heikes, Tom Christie, Scott Foundas, L.A. Weekly Film Issue</span></div>

<div><strong><span>B. TV</span></strong></div>

<div><span>B1. TV News </span></div>
<div><span>Winner:<strong>George Pennacchio</strong>, KABC/7, Oscar Pre-Show<img src="http://ih.constantcontact.com/fs007/1102748757357/img/225.jpg" border="0" alt="George Pannacchio 1" width="144" height="189"></span></div>

<div><span>B2. TV Feature/Series</span></div>
<div><span>Winner: <strong>George Pennacchio</strong>, KABC/7, Crips and Bloods</span></div>
<div><span>Second Place: George Pennacchio, KABC, Misty Upham</span></div>
<div><span>Honorable Mention: Shawn Edwards, Fox 4 News, Kansas City, Chat with the Stars</span></div>

<div><span>B3. TV Critic of the Year</span></div>
<div><span>The judges awarded no winner and encouraged TV entertainment critics to submit their most thoughtful commentary in 2011</span></div>

<div><strong><span>C. RADIO</span></strong></div>

<div><span>C1. Radio News </span></div>
<div><span>Winner: <strong>Barbara Gasser, KroneHit, Austria</strong>, Court decision on Chris Browns assault on Rihanna</span></div>

<div><span>C2. Radio Feature/Series </span></div>

<div><span>Winner: <strong>John DeSando and Kristin Dreyer Kramer</strong>, WCBE 90.5 FM, Columbus, Ohio, Its Movie Times Year in Review</span></div>
<div><span>Second Place: Brian Lauritzen and Gail Eichenthal, KUSC Los Angeles Arts Alive, Nancy Bea Hefley </span></div>
<div><span>Honorable Mention: Scott Immergut and Matt Holzman, KCRW Los Angeles The Business, Disney Convention</span></div>

<div><span>C3. Radio Critic of the Year</span></div>

<div><span>Winner:<strong>Joe Morgenstern</strong>, KCRW, Los Angeles</span></div>
<div><span>Second Place: Matt Holzman, KCRW, Los Angeles</span></div>
<div><span>Honorable Mention: John DeSando and Kristin Dreyer, WCBE 90.5 FM, Columbus, Ohio</span></div>

<div><strong><span>D. ONLINE</span></strong></div>

<div><span>D1. Online News</span></div>
<div><span>Winner:<strong>Andrew Gumbel</strong>, TheWrap.com, The Wrap Investigates the Closure of the Motion Picture &amp; Television Fund Long-term Care Center</span></div>
<div><span>Second Place: Sharon Waxman, TheWrap.com,Exclusive: Comcast in Talks to Buy Universal</span></div>
<div><span>Honorable Mention: Jeanne Wolf, Parade.com, Saying Goodbye Isnt Easy</span></div>

<div><span>D2. Online Feature/Series</span></div>
<div><span>Winner:<strong>Zachary Pincus-Roth</strong>, Slate.com, Best Weekend Never</span></div>
<div><span>Second Place: Steven Mikulan, TheWrap.com</span></div>
<div><span>Honorable Mention: Brad Schreiber, HuffingtonPost.com, Athol Fugard: Art Battling Apartheid and Aids</span></div>

<div><span>D3. Online Entertainment Critic of the Year</span></div>
<div><span>Winner: <strong>Peter Rainer</strong>, CSMonitor.com, 2009 body of work</span></div>
<div><span>Second Place: Rick Warner, Bloomberg News</span></div>
<div><span>Honorable Mention: Desson Thomson, TheWrap.com</span></div>
<div>

<p><span>D4. Entertainment Website</span></p>
<div><span>Winner:<strong>Detroit Free Press and Freep.com</strong>, Motown at 50</span></div>
<div><span>Second Place: The Wrap, TheWrap.com<br>
Honorable Mention: Shawn Edwards and Valerie Freeman, ILoveBlackMovies.com</span></div>

<div><span>D5. Entertainment Blog by an Individual</span></div>
<div><span>Winner:<strong>Tom Tangney</strong>, MyNorthwest.com</span></div>
<div><span>Second Place: Shawn Edwards, ILoveBlackMovies.com</span></div>

<div><strong><span>BEST IN SHOW, $500 Prize:</span></strong></div>
<div><span>Detroit Free Press, Freep.com, Motown at 50. </span></div>
