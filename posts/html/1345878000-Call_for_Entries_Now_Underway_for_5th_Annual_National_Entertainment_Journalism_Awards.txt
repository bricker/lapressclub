<div><strong>Los Angeles Press Club has expanded the National Entertainment Journalism Awards (NEJ) to include all arts reporters.</strong></div>
<p><strong>Categories now include nonfiction books, podcasts and documentary film.</strong></p>
<div><strong>The NEJ is the only media contest to recognize the importance of arts and entertainment reporting,  said LA Press Club President Will Lewis. It remains one of the fastest growing sections of our profession and produces some of the best writing, reporting and photography.</strong></div>
