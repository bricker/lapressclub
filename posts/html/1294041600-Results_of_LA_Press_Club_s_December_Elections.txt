Results of LA Press Club's December Elections
-----------------------------------------------

<div>
<div><span> The Los Angeles Press Club announces the results of its December election for the 2011 Board of Directors.</span></div>
<div><span>Newly elected are<strong>Tony</strong> <strong>Castro</strong>, Daily News;<strong> Eric Leonard</strong>, KFI News;<strong>Martha Sarabia</strong>, La Opinion and<strong>Sharon Waxman</strong>, The Wrap. Incumbents reelected are:<strong>John Amato</strong>, Crooks&amp;Liars;<strong>Diana Ljungaeus</strong>, international journalist and<strong>Jon Regardie</strong>, Downtown News.</span></div>
<div><span>Continuing board members are<strong>Beth Barrett</strong>, investigative reporter;<strong>Jane Engle</strong>, LA Times;<strong>Rory Johnston</strong>, freelance;<strong>Fred Mamoun</strong>, KNBC-4 News,<strong>Will Lewis</strong>, KCRW emeritus;<strong>Jill Stewart</strong>, LA Weekly and<strong>Chris Woodyard</strong>, USA Today.</span></div>
<div><span>The  incoming board represents allnews platforms-the legacy media and the  Internet, said current Club president, Will Lewis. And with the  election ofMs. Sarabria, weve added the Spanish language press.</span></div>
<div><span>The board will elect a new slate of officers at its January 18<sup>th</sup> meeting.</span></div>
<div><span>Only  members who are fulltime journalists participate in the annual  election. Active members also approved changes in the Clubs bylaws  designed to streamline board governance.</span></div>
</div>
