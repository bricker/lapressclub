How we broke the story: Gottlieb and Vives reveal the story behind the Bell City Scandal
-----------------------------------------------

<div>
<p><a href="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/08/City-of-Bell.jpg"><img title="City-of-Bell" src="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/08/City-of-Bell-150x150.jpg" alt="" width="100" height="100"></a><!--more--></p>

<div>A simple request by two <em>Los  Angeles Times</em> reporters for salary information on public officials in  the small city of Bell blossomed into a scandal that has captivated  readers nationwide. Reporters<strong> Jeff Gottlieb </strong>and <strong>Ruben Vives</strong> broke the  story that disclosed Bells city manager was being paid $787,637 a year.  Come hear them tell <strong>How we got that story</strong> next Thursday evening, Aug.  26, at the Los Angeles Press Club.</div>
