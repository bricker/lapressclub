<div>Net proceeds from the event will go to The Bill Rosendahl Fund supporting projects that further public affairs journalism. All donations are tax deductible. Bill Rosendahl, president of Los Angeles Press Club in the 1990s, and the former host of a long-running Week in Review and Talk of the Town public affairs program on Adelphia cable, launched a successful effort to revive and rebuild the Press Club during his tenure.</div>

<div>Mr. Rosendahl is currently Los Angeles City Councilman for District 11.
