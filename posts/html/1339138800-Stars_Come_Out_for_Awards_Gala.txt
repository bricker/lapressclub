Stars Come Out for Awards Gala
-----------------------------------------------

<div>
<p>When <strong>Carl Bernstein</strong> and <strong>Bob Woodward</strong> receive the Los Angeles Press Clubs Presidents Award on the 40<sup>th</sup>anniversary of Watergate at the Biltmore, the trophy will be presented by the most popular actress on TV and the man who played the president for seven years on national television.</p>
<div>
<a href="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Sheengoodforsending-1.jpg"><img title="Sheengoodforsending-1" src="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Sheengoodforsending-1-150x150.jpg" alt="" width="150" height="150"></a><p>Martin Sheen as President Bartlet on West Wing</p>
</div>
<p><strong>Pauley Perrette, </strong>star of NCIS,<strong> and Martin Sheen, </strong>actor and political activist<strong>,</strong>join a roster of noted actors and media personalities who will announce the winners of the 54<sup>th</sup> Annual Southern California Journalism Awards on Sunday, June 24, before a capacity banquet audience in the legendary Crystal Ballroom where the Oscars where originally conceived.</p>
<div>
<a href="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Pauley_Perrette_0197_Final1.jpg"><img title="Pauley_Perrette_0197_Final" src="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/06/Pauley_Perrette_0197_Final1-150x150.jpg" alt="" width="150" height="150"></a><p>NCIS Pauley Perrette</p>
</div>
<p>Other actors and news anchors who will present the awards for the Southlands best journalism of 2011 across all news platforms are: <strong>Patt Morrison, Dan Lauria, Jodi Long, Pat Harvey, Ron Perlman </strong>and<strong> Robert Kovacik.</strong> Returning as emcee for a third straight year is <strong>Jack Maxwell.</strong></p>
<p>Winners will have been chosen by press clubs across the nation from a record 750 entries in one of the oldest and largest journalism competitionsnot just in Southern California, but the nation as well.</p>
