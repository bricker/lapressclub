<p>Dont miss out on National Entertainment Journalism Awards Party and Dinner.</p>
<p>Your ticket gives you a chance to rub elbows with top journalists in entertainment media  and some of Hollywoods royalty.</p>
<p>Rising star writer/director Scott Cooper, the creative force behind <em>Crazy Heart</em> and the upcoming <em>Out of the Furnace</em> will present the Visionary Award to Forest Whitaker who is being honored for his humanitarian work.</p>
<p>L.A. Film Festivals David Ansen will present Los Angeles Times and NPR film critic Kenneth Turan with the Luminary Award for Career Achievement.</p>
<p>The celebration takes place in the Crystal Ballroom at Millennium Biltmore Hotel, downtown Los Angeles.</p>
<p>It all starts at <strong>4:30 pm</strong> on the Red Carpet, <strong>5 pm</strong> cocktails and Silent Auction where you can win trips, dinners, art and jewelry. The three-course dinner is served at <strong>6 pm.</strong> Be there!</p>
