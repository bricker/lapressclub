Hear the Roar of Iran's Lion Women --  Thursday Night
-----------------------------------------------

<div>
<p><strong><em><a href="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/Lion-Women-copy.jpg"><img title="Lion Women copy" src="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/06/Lion-Women-copy-300x176.jpg" alt="" width="152" height="89"></a>Lion Women </em></strong>chronicles the untold story of the Iranian womens movement and its fight for democracy and human rights  which culminated a year ago in Teherans bloody streets as the Lion Women  joined the hundreds of thousands protesting the controversial presidential  election. For the first time Iranian women reveal to the world their heartbreaking  stories in this powerful documentary. Directed by press club director <strong>Gry Winther </strong>who stood shoulder to shoulder with the  Lion Women amid the violence. There will be a Q and A after this press club screening with Ms. Winther and a representative from the One Million Signatures Campaign. <a href="http://lionwomen.eventbrite.com" target="_blank">R.S.V.P.</a></p>
