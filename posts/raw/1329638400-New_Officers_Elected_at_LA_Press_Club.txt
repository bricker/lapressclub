New Officers Elected at LA Press Club
-----------------------------------------------

<div class="post-bodycopy clearfix">
<p>Los Angeles Press Club board has elected its officers for 2012.</p>
<p><strong>Will Lewis</strong>, KCRW emeritus, was re-elected president; <strong>Martha Sarabia</strong>, LA Opinion, is vice president; <strong>Beth Barrett</strong>, investigative reporter and formerly vice president, is treasurer; <strong>Jane Engle,</strong> former LA Times travel editor, was re-elected secretary. <strong>Diana Ljungaeus</strong> continues as executive director.</p>
