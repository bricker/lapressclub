<p>Governor<strong> Arnold Schwarzenegger</strong> delivers the opening remarks at the 52nd Annual SoCal Journalism Awards Dinner on June 27.</p>
<p><a href="http://socal52.eventbrite.com" target="_blank">Join us</a> to honor <strong>Anderson Cooper</strong> (CNN/60 Minutes) — President’s Award; <strong>Dave Bryan </strong>(CBS2/KCAL) — Joseph M Quinn Award and <strong>Anne Garrels</strong> (NPR) — Daniel Pearl Award.</p>
<p>Presenters  include <strong>Sean Penn, Pat Harvey, Ana Garcia, Stana Katic</strong> (ABC’s Castle), <strong>Wendie Malick</strong> (Hot in Cleveland) and <strong>George Pennacchio</strong>. <a href="http://socal52.eventbrite.com" target="_blank"><strong> Tickets</strong></a></p>
