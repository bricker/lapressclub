How does media cover the wealthy?
-----------------------------------------------

<div class="post-bodycopy clearfix">
<p>How does media cover the wealthy? Learn from the experts.</p>
<p>Panel discussion at the Press Club, Thursday, February 27.</p>
<p>Panelists:</p>
<p>PETER NEWCOMB, Bloomberg News editor who leads Bloomberg’s Billionaires coverage, and who used to oversee the Forbes 400 list of richest Americans.</p>
<p>SETH LUBOVE, Sitrick and Co., former L.A. bureau chief at Bloomberg News and Forbes. Seth uncovered the youngest female billionaire in America, 30-year-old Lynsi Torres, who inherited the In-N-Out hamburger chain. Now he advises clients on how to respond to reporters’ inquiries.</p>
<p>DOROTHY POMERANTZ, Forbes, who runs the annual Celebrity 100 list of highest-paid stars in Hollywood.</p>
<p>JAMES RUFUS KOREN, L.A. Business Journal reporter who discovered an under-the-radar car-finance billionaire, Don Hankey.</p>
<p>Moderator:<br>
ANTHONY PALAZZO, Los Angeles Press Club officer and current Bloomberg Bureau Chief in Los Angeles.</p>
<p>Doors open: 6:30 pm<br>
Networking and finger food.<br>
Program starts at 7.30 pm.<br>
Free Parking, enter from Berendo.<br>
Metro: Red Line Sunset/Vermont</p>
