Call for Entries Now Underway for 5th Annual National Entertainment Journalism Awards
-----------------------------------------------

<h4><strong><em><span style="color: #666699;">Arts &amp; Entertainment Journalists In All Media To Be Honored For Their Best Work in 2011-12</span></em></strong></h4>
<p><strong><em></em></strong><a href="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/NEJ_logo1.jpg"><img class="size-thumbnail wp-image-1748 alignleft" title="NEJ_logo(1)" src="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2011/10/NEJ_logo1-150x150.jpg" alt="" width="150" height="150"></a></p>
<div><strong>Los Angeles Press Club has expanded the National Entertainment Journalism Awards (NEJ) to include all arts reporters.</strong></div>
<p><strong>Categories now include nonfiction books, podcasts and documentary film.</strong></p>
<div><strong>“The NEJ is the only media contest to recognize the importance of arts and entertainment reporting, “ said LA Press Club President Will Lewis. “It remains one of the fastest growing sections of our profession and produces some of the best writing, reporting and photography.”</strong></div>
<div><span style="color: #ffffff;">xxxx</span></div>
<div><strong>Winners will be announced and honored at an Awards Gala, Sunday, Nov. 18 at the Millennium Biltmore.</strong></div>
