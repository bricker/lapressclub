Former Club President Dick Turpin Dies
-----------------------------------------------

<div class="post-bodycopy clearfix">
<p><a href="http://ow.ly/3oCpv" target="popupAd"></a><span id="more-1146"></span></p>
<p><strong><a href="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/dick-turpin582271141.jpg"></a><a href="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/dick-turpin582271142.jpg"><img class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-1152" title="dick turpin58227114" src="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/dick-turpin582271142-150x150.jpg" alt="" width="150" height="150"></a>LA Press Club is saddened by the death on Friday of</strong> <strong>Dick Turpin, a former Club president and longtime board member and supporter.</strong><br>
<strong>He is remembered for his 41 years at the LA Times for his time as a reporter, education editor and real estate editor. He was on the Watts riots team that won a Pulitzer Prize. <a href="http://www.latimes.com/news/obituaries/la-me-dick-turpin-20101212,0,6073085.story" target="_blank">MORE</a></strong></p>

<p>Dear friends,</p>
<div>
<p>As you know, Dick Turpin passed away last month.</p>
</div>
<div>
<p>During his 91 years, he rose from humble roots to live an extraordinary life that was filled with both substantial challenges and remarkable accomplishments. We’ve planned a memorial in his honor to celebrate his life, and we hope you can all attend.</p>
