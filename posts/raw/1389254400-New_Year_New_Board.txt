New Year, New Board
-----------------------------------------------

<div class="post-bodycopy clearfix">
<hr>
<div><strong><a href="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Robert-Kovacik.jpg"><img class="alignleft size-thumbnail wp-image-4210" title="Robert Kovacik" src="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/Robert-Kovacik-150x150.jpg" alt="" width="150" height="150"></a></strong></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<div><strong>Los Angeles Press Club has elected a new board and officers:</strong></div>
<div></div>
<div>
<strong>President: </strong>Robert Kovacik, <em>NBC4 LA</em><br>
<strong>Vice President:</strong> Patt Morrison. <em>Los Angeles Times / KPCC</em><br>
<strong>Secretary:</strong> Christina Villacorte, <em>L.A. Daily News /</em> Gloria Zuurveen, <em>PACE News</em><br>
<strong>Treasurer:</strong> Anthony Palazzo, <em>Bloomberg News</em>
</div>
<div>
<p>Carolina Sarassa, <em>MundoFox</em>, Fernando Mexia, <em>EFE News</em> and Jahan Hassan, <em>Ekush News</em> are new board members.</p>
<p> </p>
<hr>
<div></div>
<div><a href="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/NewBoardMembers2014.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-4188 alignnone" title="NewBoardMembers2014" src="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/NewBoardMembers2014-600x144.jpg" alt="" width="600" height="144"></a></div>
<div>Reelected to serve another two years are <strong>Barbara Gasser</strong>, freelance journalist,  <strong>Gloria Zuurveen</strong>, Publisher of <em>Pace News</em> and <strong>Robert Kovacik</strong>, anchor/reporter at <em>NBC News</em> in Los Angeles.</div>
<div>Remaining on the board for another year are <strong>Maria Armoudian</strong>, <em>KPFK,</em> <strong> Diana Ljungaeus, </strong>International Journalist, <strong>Patt Morrison, </strong><em>LA Times/ KPCC, </em><strong>Tony Pierce,</strong> <em>AMPAS,</em> and <strong>Christina Villacorte, </strong><em>L.A. Daily News.</em>
</div>
<div><em> </em></div>
<div>We thank <strong>Beth Barrett, Jane Engle, Will Lewis</strong> and <strong>Jill Stewart</strong> for their six years of invaluable service on the board and hope to see them return in the future.</div>
