Nat'l Entertainment Journalism Awards Gala, Nov. 24

<p><strong>Forest Whitaker</strong> — <em>Visionary Award</em>, introduced by <strong>Scott Cooper</strong>; <strong>Kenneth Turan</strong> — <em>Luminary Award</em>, introduced by<strong> David Ansen</strong>; <strong>Graydon Carter</strong> — <em>Special Centennial Award</em></p>
<p><span style="color: #800080;"><strong><a href="http://shop.lapressclub.info/category.php?id_category=9"><span style="color: #800080;">Buy Tickets</span></a></strong> </span>— <strong><span style="color: #800080;"><a href="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/Finalists12.pdf"><span style="color: #800080;">Finalists</span></a></span><span style="color: #800080;"><span style="color: #800080;"> — </span></span><span style="color: #800080;"><a href="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/10/2013-invite-NEJ1.pdf"><span style="color: #800080;">Invite</span></a></span></strong></p>
<p>Don’t miss out on National Entertainment Journalism Awards Party and Dinner.</p>
<p>Your ticket gives you a chance to rub elbows with top journalists in entertainment media – and some of Hollywood’s royalty.</p>
<p>Rising star writer/director Scott Cooper, the creative force behind <em>Crazy Heart</em> and the upcoming <em>Out of the Furnace</em> will present the Visionary Award to Forest Whitaker who is being honored for his humanitarian work.</p>
<p>L.A. Film Festival’s David Ansen will present Los Angeles Times and NPR film critic Kenneth Turan with the Luminary Award for Career Achievement.</p>
<p>The celebration takes place in the Crystal Ballroom at Millennium Biltmore Hotel, downtown Los Angeles.</p>
<p>It all starts at <strong>4:30 pm</strong> on the Red Carpet, <strong>5 pm</strong> cocktails and Silent Auction where you can win trips, dinners, art and jewelry.  The three-course dinner is served at <strong>6 pm.</strong>  Be there!</p>
