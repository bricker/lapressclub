<p>Stars keep coming out for the NEJ awards gala Nov. 18 at the Biltmore.</p>
<p><strong>Robert Redford</strong> will be on hand to introduce Visionary Award honoree <strong>Jane Fonda.</strong></p>
<p><strong>Jeff Daniels</strong>, star of The Newsroom, will make a special appearance. Ms. Fonda sits down for a candid, onstage interview with NBC4 and LAPC board member <strong>Robert Kovacik.</strong></p>
<p>Comedian, author and talk show star <strong>Kathy Griffin</strong> will do the introduction to The Hollywood Reporter’s <strong>Janice Min </strong>who receives a career achievement award from the Press Club.</p>
<p>And that’s just who are onstage. Red Carpet caliber personalities will be scattered throughout the legendary Crystal Ballroom.</p>
<p>Emcee <strong>Jack Maxwell</strong>.</p>
