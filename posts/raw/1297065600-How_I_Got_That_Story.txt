How I Got That Story
-----------------------------------------------

<div class="post-bodycopy clearfix">
<div id="attachment_1254" class="wp-caption alignleft" style="width: 160px">
<a href="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/Alexik-02.jpg"><img class="size-thumbnail wp-image-1254" title="Alexik 02" src="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2011/02/Alexik-02-150x150.jpg" alt="" width="150" height="150"></a><p class="wp-caption-text">Bono and Alexik</p>
</div>
<p>On  April 19, 2010 suspected counterfeiter, drug dealer and weapons maker <strong>Brian  Alexik</strong> evaded police at the door of his ritzy Downtown penthouse — which  overlooked the L.A. branch of the Federal Reserve bank — by sneaking  down a fire escape. On June 3, he was arrested in a rundown apartment  barely a mile from his home. In the following months, he opened up to <em>Los Angeles Downtown News</em> reporter <strong>Ryan Vaillancourt</strong>, who had been the first to cover the story.  The two spoke repeatedly on the phone and, Alexik sent letters, and Vaillancourt visited him in prison.<span id="more-1253"></span></p>
<p>On Feb. 10, Vaillancourt will discuss the jailhouse mind of a person the LAPD  warned should be considered armed and dangerous. But that’s just the  beginning of the story for a man who lived the high life, partied with  Bono and is representing himself in court.</p>
<p>Drinks, snacks and networking at 7 pm.</p>
<p>Program at 7:30</p>
<p>Free Parking</p>
