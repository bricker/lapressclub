<h2 style="text-align: center;" align="center"><strong><span style="color: #ff0000;">COME ONE, COME ALL</span></strong></h2>
<h2 style="text-align: center;" align="center">
<img class="size-medium wp-image-4162 alignleft" title="Screen Shot 2013-12-04 at 12.07.14 PM" src="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/12/Screen-Shot-2013-12-04-at-12.07.14-PM-57x300.png" alt="" width="57" height="300"><strong><span style="font-size: 13px;">Join us as we celebrate the holiday season!</span> </strong>
</h2>
<p style="text-align: center;" align="center"><strong>Press Photographers Association of Greater Los Angeles<br>
</strong><strong>and<br>
</strong><strong>the</strong><strong> Los Angeles Press Club<br>
</strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;" align="center">Invite our members to come together to kick off the holiday season on Friday December 13th</p>
<p style="text-align: center;" align="center">6:00pm — 10:00pm at the Glendale Moose Lodge.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;" align="center">————————————</p>
<p style="text-align: center;" align="center">We also want to unite and ask our members to share the joy of Christmas</p>
<p style="text-align: center;" align="center">and bring an unwrapped toy (value up to $20) to our evening event.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">                The Toys for Tots collection box will be on site Friday evening and will also be available for toy drop off<br>
on Saturday, December 14th from 11am to 3pm.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>                            </strong><strong>We look forward to seeing you!</strong><strong></strong></p>
<p style="text-align: center;">                                    The Glendale Moose Lodge is located at 357 W. Arden Avenue, Glendale, CA 91203.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">                  PPAGLA and LA Press Club will provide appetizers.</p>
<p style="text-align: center;">                  The Moose Lodge will have their dining room and bar open for service.</p>
