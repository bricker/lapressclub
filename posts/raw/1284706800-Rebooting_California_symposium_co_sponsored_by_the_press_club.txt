Rebooting California, symposium co-sponsored by the press club
-----------------------------------------------

<div class="post-bodycopy clearfix">
<p><a href="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/reboot_ca_plate.jpg"><img class="alignleft size-full wp-image-1006" title="reboot_ca_plate" src="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/09/reboot_ca_plate.jpg" alt="" width="240" height="101"></a></p>
<ul>
<li>Be better equipped to cover California Politics — enjoy a full day of symposium panels at Loyola Law School on Friday, September 24th. Experts explore how to fix what’s wrong with the Golden State. Former Gov. Gray Davis is the luncheon speaker. LA Press Club is a proud co-sponsor. The event is free, but you have to <a href="http://rebootca.org/register.html" target="_blank">REGISTER</a>. For a full schedule and list of panelists <a href="http://rebootca.org/schedule.html" target="_blank">CLICK HERE.</a>
</li>
</ul>
