Meet Thom Hartmann at the Press Club on Saturday 2/2
-----------------------------------------------

<div class="post-bodycopy clearfix">
<h5>
<span style="color: navy; font-family: Arial; font-size: medium;">Join us for a conversation about radio journalism with Thom Hartmann, radio host who presents “Renaissance thinking about the issues of our day.”</span><span style="color: navy; font-family: Arial; font-size: medium;"> </span>
</h5>
<h5><span style="font-family: Arial;">Hartmann is a progressive political commentator and former psychotherapist and entrepreneur.</span></h5>

<p> </p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;">This is event is a coproduction between KPFK an Los Angeles Press Club.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;">Saturday, February 2 at 2pm - <a title="Register" href="http://thomhartmann.eventbrite.com">Register Here</a></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;">Los Angeles Press Club, 4773 Hollywood Blvd., L.A. CA 90027. !</span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial;">Plenty of parking in the back of the building.</span></p>
