How we broke the story: Gottlieb and Vives reveal the story behind the Bell City Scandal
-----------------------------------------------

<div class="post-bodycopy clearfix">
<p><a href="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/08/City-of-Bell.jpg"><img class="alignright size-thumbnail wp-image-977" title="City-of-Bell" src="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2010/08/City-of-Bell-150x150.jpg" alt="" width="100" height="100"></a><span id="more-964"></span><!--more--></p>
<p><span style="font-family: Arial,sans-serif; font-size: small;"> </span></p>
<div>A simple request by two <em>Los  Angeles Times</em> reporters for salary information on public officials in  the small city of Bell blossomed into a scandal that has captivated  readers nationwide. Reporters<strong> Jeff Gottlieb </strong>and <strong>Ruben Vives</strong> broke the  story that disclosed Bell’s city manager was being paid $787,637 a year.  Come hear them tell <strong>“How we got that story”</strong> next Thursday evening, Aug.  26, at the Los Angeles Press Club.</div>
<div><a href="http://bellscoop.eventbrite.com" target="_blank">R.S.V.P. here</a></div>
<div><span><br>
</span></div>
<div>When: 7 p.m., Thursday, Aug. 26</div>
<div>Where:  Los Angeles Press Club in the Steve Allen Theater, 4773 Hollywood Blvd.  (two blocks west of Vermont), Metro: Red Line, Sunset/Vermont</div>
<div>Cost: Free for members, $15 for nonmembers…refreshments included, of course.</div>
