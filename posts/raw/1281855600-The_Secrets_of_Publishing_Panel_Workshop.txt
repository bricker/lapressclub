</span> <span style="font-family: Geneva; color: #000000; font-size: small;"><strong>MORE  ABOUT SUE:</strong></span><span style="font-size: small;"> </span><span style="font-family: Geneva; color: #000000; font-size: small;">Susan Shapiro,  a Manhattan journalism professor, has written for the New York Times, Washington Post, L.A. She’s sold two novels</span><span style="font-size: small;"> </span><span style="font-family: Geneva; color: #000000; font-size: small;"><em>Overexposed</em></span><span style="font-size: small;"> </span><span style="font-family: Geneva; color: #000000; font-size: small;">and</span> <span style="font-family: Geneva; color: #000000; font-size: small;"><em>Speed Shrinking</em></span><span style="font-size: small;">.</span><span style="font-family: Geneva; color: #000000; font-size: small;"> She  lives with her husband, a TV/film writer, in Greenwich Village, where she teaches her own “instant gratification takes too long” writing method at the New School, NYU and in private workshops and seminars.</span></span></p>
