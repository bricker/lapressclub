4th National Entertainment Awards
-----------------------------------------------

<div class="post-bodycopy clearfix">
<p><strong><span style="text-decoration: underline;"><em> Final deadline extended to Tuesday, October 11; Diana must approve extensions. 310-210-1860 or diana@lapressclub.org<br>
</em></span></strong></p>
<p><strong><a href="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/NEJ_logo4inFin-11.jpg"><img class="alignleft size-medium wp-image-1668" title="NEJ_logo" src="http://lapressclub.org/wp-content/uploads/2011/08/NEJ_logo4inFin-11-300x300.jpg" alt="" width="197" height="197"></a>This national contest is aimed at entertainment reporters and editors, film and television critics across the country, including foreign journalists. Open to all U.S.-based entertainment journalists for work published or disseminated during 2010/2011 (January 1 2010 – June 30 2011). All categories judged and awarded separately. Entries in a foreign language must include a translation.</strong></p>
<p><strong>New this year: awards for best use of social media.</strong></p>
<p><strong>One cash prize of $500 is awarded to Entertainment Journalist of the Year.</strong></p>
