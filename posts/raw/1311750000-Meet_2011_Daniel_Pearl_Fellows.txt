<blockquote>
<p><span style="color: #000000;"> </span></p>
<p>We are pleased to invite you to meet with our 2011 Daniel Pearl Fellows, Aatekah Mir from Pakistan and Emal Haidary from Afghanistan. They will share their experiences in America at a panel discussion moderated by Rob Eshman, Thursday, August 25, 2011, 7:30 p.m. at the Los Angeles Press Club. The reception starts at 7:00 p.m.<!--more--></p>
<p>Established in 2003, in partnership with the <a href="http://www.pressfellowships.org/">Alfred Friendly Press Fellowships</a>, the <a href="http://www.pressfellowships.org/DPearl.html">Daniel Pearl Fellows</a> come from countries in the Middle East, North Africa and South Asia, which Danny covered as a journalist. They work for six months at major U.S. newsrooms and experience the dynamics of a free press. Each Fellow also spends one week at a Jewish publication.</p>
<p><strong>We are hosting the following two journalists:</strong></p>
<p><img src="http://www.danielpearl.org/images/2011DPFAatekahAhhmadMirweb.jpg" alt="" align="left"><strong>Aatekah Ahhmad Mir</strong> (30) from Pakistan, is currently working in New York at the <em>Wall Street Journal</em>. In Lahore, she is a senior sub-editor for <em>The Express Tribune</em>, a partner of the <em>International Herald Tribune</em>, based in Pakistan.</p>
<p><img src="http://www.danielpearl.org/images/2011DPFMohammadEmalNaweedHaidaryweb.jpg" alt="" align="left"><strong>Emal Naweed Haidary</strong> (31) from Afghanistan, is currently working at the <em>Los Angeles Times</em>. In Kabul, he is a reporter for the Bokhdi News Agency and recently started working as editor for <em>Afghan Voices</em>, an organization that trains young Afghan journalists.</p>
<p><strong><!--more--></strong></p>
<p><strong>Moderator:</strong></p>
<p><img src="http://www.danielpearl.org/images/Robeshman.jpg" alt="" align="left"><strong>Rob Eshman</strong>, Editor in Chief, <em><a href="http://www.jewishjournal.com/about/author/2835/">The Jewish Journal of Greater Los Angeles</a></em>, has moderated these public discussions annually since 2004.</p>
<p>The event will take place at:</p>
<blockquote><p>Steve Allen Theater<br>
4773 Hollywood Boulevard<br>
Los Angeles, CA 90027-5333<br>
(Parking is available in the back of the building)</p></blockquote>
<p>Admission is free and seating is limited. If you would like to attend, please <a href="http://www.danielpearl.org/fellowstickets.html">click here for tickets</a>. If you encounter a problem with the online registration, call us at (310) 441-1400 to reserve tickets.</p>
<p>Our deep gratitude to the <a href="http://www.lapressclub.org/">Los Angeles Press Club</a> for hosting this event. We look forward to seeing you there.</p>
<p>Best regards,</p>
<p>Judea and Ruth Pearl<br>
Daniel Pearl Foundation<br>
<a href="http://www.danielpearl.org/">www.danielpearl.org</a></p>
