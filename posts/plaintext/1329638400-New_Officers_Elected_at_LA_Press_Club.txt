New Officers Elected at LA Press Club
-----------------------------------------------

Los Angeles Press Club board has elected its officers for 2012.

Will Lewis, KCRW emeritus, was re-elected president; Martha Sarabia, LA Opinion, is vice president; Beth Barrett, investigative reporter and formerly vice president, is treasurer; Jane Engle, former LA Times travel editor, was re-elected secretary. Diana Ljungaeus continues as executive director.
