Behind the Grim Sleeper Scoop
-----------------------------------------------

Award-winning journalists Christine Pelisek and Jill Stewart, discuss “How I Got the Story,”  their fascinating account of the Grim Sleeper serial killer coverage at the Los Angeles Weekly.  THIS EVENT IS POSTPONED. Please check back for a new time.                 R.S.V.P.Pelisek, who recently moved to The Daily Beast, and Stewart, news editor of L.A. Weekly, will talk about the three years they worked together as reporter and editor on a series of stories about the Grim Sleeper, who Pelisek doggedly tracked — sometimes knowing even more clues than the cops. 

7 pm — networking, drinks and fingerfood

7.30pm — program


Free parking, enter from Berendo.

Metro: Red Line Sunset/Vermont
