Arnold Schwarzenegger  joins star studded gala line-up
-----------------------------------------------

Governor Arnold Schwarzenegger delivers the opening remarks at the 52nd Annual SoCal Journalism Awards Dinner on June 27.

Join us to honor Anderson Cooper (CNN/60 Minutes) — President’s Award; Dave Bryan (CBS2/KCAL) — Joseph M Quinn Award and Anne Garrels (NPR) — Daniel Pearl Award.

Presenters  include Sean Penn, Pat Harvey, Ana Garcia, Stana Katic (ABC’s Castle), Wendie Malick (Hot in Cleveland) and George Pennacchio.  Tickets
